Secondary structure
Home Primary conversion hub.
Workshops Overview of workshop formats, audiences, outcomes, duration options, and booking process.
Programs Two child pages:
* Coding with Robots
* PSTEM
Resources Curated public samples only:
* Sample Activities
* Parent / Educator Info
* FAQs
About Brand story, credentials, delivery approach, partnerships, staff credibility.
Book a Workshop Dedicated booking/lead page with form, workshop options, audience selector, and next steps.
Contact Simple direct contact page

What gets removed or merged
* Order becomes Book a Workshop
* Curriculum gets removed from top nav and its useful content gets folded into Programs/Resources
* Professional Development gets removed as a standalone nav item unless you later confirm real content for it
* Downloads becomes a cleaner Resources page
That structure is justified by the current site: Curriculum and Professional Development have little or no meaningful content, while Downloads contains the useful material and Order is currently just a lightweight quote step.
