Session Formats

Built Around Your Schedule

We offer three flexible formats — each delivering the same expert-led, hands-on quality, adapted to the time you have available.

~2 Hours

Half-Day Session

A focused, high-energy workshop that introduces one program and takes students through a complete investigation or coding challenge from start to finish.

  • One program: Coding with Robots or PSTEM
  • Ideal for single-class groups
  • Everything set up and packed down by our team
~4–5 Hours

Full-Day Session

A deeper dive that gives students time to explore, refine their thinking, and tackle more complex challenges — including a mix of both programs where group size allows.

  • One or two programs across the day
  • Ideal for whole-year or multi-class groups
  • Structured breaks; pacing matched to student energy
Multiple Sessions

Programme Series

For schools and groups who want a sustained STEM experience — a series of sessions over weeks or a term, building skills and confidence progressively.

  • Curriculum-aligned and term-plan ready
  • Progressive skill build across all four coding levels
  • Ideal for schools with dedicated STEM time
