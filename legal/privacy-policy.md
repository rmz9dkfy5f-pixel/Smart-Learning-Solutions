# Privacy Policy

**Effective date:** 2026-06-23
**Last updated:** 2026-06-23

Smart Learning Solutions ("we," "us," "our") provides hands-on, in-person STEM workshops
for K–12 students. This policy explains what information we collect through this website,
why we collect it, who we share it with, and the choices you have.

This website is intended to be used by adults — parents, teachers, and community
organisers — who are arranging workshops on behalf of students. We do not knowingly
collect personal information directly from children through this website.

## 2. Information we collect

### a. Information you give us through our forms

When you submit our **Request a Workshop** form, we collect:

- First and last name
- Email address
- Phone number (optional)
- School or organisation name (optional)
- Preferred program (Coding with Robots / PSTEM / not sure yet)
- Approximate group size
- Age or grade range of the group
- Preferred date(s) (optional)
- Location or venue (optional)
- Any additional message you choose to send

When you submit our **Contact** form, we collect:

- Your name
- Email address
- Your message

We only collect what you choose to enter. The age/grade range and group size describe a
group in general terms; we do not ask for, and you should not submit, the personal details
of individual children through these forms.

### b. Information collected automatically

We use **privacy-friendly, cookie-free analytics** (Plausible Analytics) to understand how
the site is used. This does **not** use cookies, does **not** track you across other
websites, and does **not** collect or store information that personally identifies you.
We see only aggregated data such as page views, referring sites, approximate country,
browser, and device type.

When your browser loads the site, certain third parties (listed in Section 4) may receive
your IP address as a normal part of delivering fonts and scripts. We do not control how
those providers process that information beyond what their own policies state.

We do **not** use advertising cookies or third-party marketing trackers.

---

## 3. How we use your information

We use the information you provide to:

- Respond to your enquiry and follow up about a workshop
- Prepare a quote tailored to your group and requirements
- Arrange and deliver a booked workshop
- Keep a record of our correspondence with you

We do **not** sell your personal information, and we do **not** use it for advertising.

**Legal basis (where applicable):** we process this information to take steps at your
request before entering into an arrangement (responding to your enquiry) and for our
legitimate interest in operating and improving our services.

## 7. Your choices and rights

Depending on where you live, you may have the right to:

- Access the personal information we hold about you
- Correct inaccurate information
- Request deletion of your information
- Object to or restrict certain processing
- Request a copy of your information in a portable format

To exercise any of these, email **info@SmartLearningSolutions.org**. We will respond within
the timeframe required by the laws that apply to you.

If you are in the European Economic Area or the United Kingdom, you also have the right to
lodge a complaint with your local data protection authority.
